The problem

Every new job brings
hundreds of CVs

You’re juggling multiple roles and urgent deadlines. With every new job opening, you receive piles of CVs – and barely have time to read them.

After a hire is made, the rest are forgotten. Valuable profiles, lost in inboxes or folders.

⚙️ The solution

Let AI do the heavy lifting

Just upload a CV – Selekt instantly summarizes the candidate, extracts key skills, and organizes the data for you.

No manual copy-paste, no formatting. It’s fast, structured, and ready to use.

♻️ The long-term win

Find top talent faster by
reusing your own CVs

Next time you recruit, just search your own AI-sorted database. Filter by skills, role or experience and rediscover great candidates you’ve already vetted.

Selekt helps you save time, avoid double work – and never waste a good CV again.
